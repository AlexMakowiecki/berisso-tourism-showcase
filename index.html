Visit Berisso

Enjoy the culture and the water in this vibrant city in Buenos Aires.

Top three activities to do at Berisso

Take a boat trip

You can take a tour in a boat and visit the nearby isles

Go to the immigrant festival

If you visit on September 15th you can go to the central park and enjoy the immigrant festival!

Go for a kayak ride

If you want to do any sport, you can try with the kayak
